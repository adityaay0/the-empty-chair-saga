# The Mitra Mandal Chronicles

> From Four Dreamers To A Civilization

---

## Overview

The Mitra Mandal Chronicles is a fictional multi-generational political and civilizational saga spanning over 150 years.

The story follows the rise of Mitra Mandal from a small community group founded beneath a banyan tree to the most influential civic institution in human history.

At the center of the story lies a mysterious symbol:

The Empty Chair.
